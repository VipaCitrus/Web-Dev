WHY DUNKIN?

Dunkin' originated from actress Mae Murray's

accidental discovery of the delicious combination of coffee and donuts at Liny's Deli in New York's Broadway.

This led to the term "dunk-in," inspiring the creation

of the Dunkin' brand, known for its diverse coffee

and donut offerings.
